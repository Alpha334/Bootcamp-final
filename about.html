About CodeCraft Academy

We're a team of passionate engineers and educators on a mission to make tech careers accessible to everyone.

Our Story

CodeCraft Academy was born from a simple observation: traditional computer science degrees take too long, cost too much, and often fail to prepare students for real-world development work.

In 2019, our founders — three senior engineers who had hired (and fired) dozens of bootcamp grads — decided to build the program they wished existed. One that teaches the skills actually used in production, with the intensity and support needed to truly transform careers.

Today, we've helped over 500 students launch careers in tech. Our graduates work at startups, Fortune 500s, and everything in between. But our proudest metric isn't the job placement rate — it's the number of alumni who come back to mentor new cohorts.

Our Teaching Philosophy

We believe the best way to learn code is to write code — lots of it, with expert guidance and real feedback.

🎯

Learn by Building

Every concept is taught through a real project. No toy examples — you'll ship actual applications you can put in your portfolio from day one.

🤝

Collaboration Over Competition

Software is a team sport. We pair program, do code reviews, and build a culture where helping each other succeed is the norm.

🔄

Iterate & Improve

Your first draft won't be perfect — and that's the point. We teach you to embrace feedback, refactor ruthlessly, and grow with every commit.

🌱

Career-Ready, Not Just Code-Ready

Technical skills get you the interview. Communication, teamwork, and problem-solving get you the job. We teach both.
